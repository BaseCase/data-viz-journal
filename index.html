Human-Centered Data Visualization

About

Hello! This website is a log of my experience doing an independent study project about data visualizations as part of the Madison College Graphic Design program. This site is just a blog; the final product will be linked here once it exists.

Fast Facts

  • Who - Casey (student)
  • Who Else - Phillip McKeown (faculty advisor)
  • What - A study of data visualization techniques and an inquiry into how they might be pushed further to enable developing intuition about complicated data
  • When - Fall 2021
  • Where - Madison College
  • Why - I think innumeracy is a huge problem and leads to bad policy decisions as a direct result of people trusting their poor intuition about big numbers
